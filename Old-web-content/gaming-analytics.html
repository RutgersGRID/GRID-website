Gaming Analytics allow a data-driven interpretation of how game applications perform, player behavior, interactions, and session data collection.

Game applications often involve the collection of data at different stages, whether you need a username to login to a game, capture questions and answers while players interact or play the game, make decisions, or to fill out a survey at the end of the game, data collection enables you to analyze the player engagement, contextualize the results,  measure performance, and more.

In a educational learning environment, data analytics can provide insights into how your students interact with your educational game, course, activity or training and possibly the effectiveness of your game. This information and feedback can help you make better educational design decisions.

The Gaming Analytics service is provided on demand as part of its educational game design and development cycle. We can provide analytics and insights for games developed using the following technologies:

  • JavaScript
  • Unity Game Engine
  • Articulate 360 Storyline
