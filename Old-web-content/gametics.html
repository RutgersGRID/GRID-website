Gametics is part of GRID's Gaming Analytics service. Gametics is an online tool that allows game owners to view their insights, statistics and metrics about their games and how people is interacting with them.

The idea behind Gametics is to have a centralized application to view your data. No more email attachments or reports, Gametics will provide you access to view your data through easy-to-use dashboards and views.
