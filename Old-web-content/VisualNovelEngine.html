Reimagine Education Through Interactive Storytelling

GRID VNE is a powerful yet intuitive platform designed specifically for educators who want to create engaging, branching narrative experiences for their students without the technical barriers traditionally associated with game development.

Visual novels are interactive fiction games that combine text-based storytelling with visual elements, allowing players to make choices that affect the narrative's direction. Originally developed for educational purposes in Japan, visual novels have evolved into a versatile medium for teaching complex concepts through immersive storytelling.

Why Visual Novels for Education?

Low Resource Requirements

Create compelling educational experiences without large teams, extensive technical skills, or significant time investments.

Active Learning

Transform passive content consumption into active decision-making that promotes critical thinking and deeper engagement.

Consequence Exploration

Allow students to safely explore the outcomes of different decisions in simulated scenarios relevant to your field.

Measurable Outcomes

Collect detailed data on how students navigate through scenarios to assess understanding and decision-making processes.

Key Features of GRID VNE

Spreadsheet-Based Creation

Design your entire game using familiar Google Sheets. Simply fill in dialogue, narration, character appearances, backgrounds, and sound effects — GRID VNE handles all the technical integration.

Integrated Journal System

Prompt students with reflection questions after key events. Journal entries are saved and can be revisited, creating a personalized record of each student's unique journey and learning process.

Strategic Rewind Points

Create checkpoints that allow students to revisit critical decision points, encouraging exploration of different approaches and outcomes.

Comprehensive Analytics

Gain valuable insights into how students navigate your scenarios with detailed data collection on choices, time spent, and paths taken.

Canvas LMS Integration

Seamlessly embed your visual novels directly into Canvas, making them easily accessible to students within your existing course environment.

Included Hosting

Focus on teaching, not technical details. We provide reliable hosting for all your visual novel games at no additional cost.

The Educational Roots of Visual Novels

1980s

Early text-based educational adventures emerge in Japan, focusing on teaching language and problem-solving skills.

1990s

Visual novels evolve with the addition of graphics, creating more immersive learning environments for students.

2000s

The genre expands globally, with educators recognizing their potential for teaching ethics, history, and complex decision-making.

2010s

Visual novels gain academic recognition as effective tools for scenario-based learning across disciplines.

Today

GRID VNE makes this powerful educational tool accessible to all educators, regardless of technical background.
