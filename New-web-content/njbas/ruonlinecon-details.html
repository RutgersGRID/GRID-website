RUOnlineCon 2019 - Passport to eXtended Realities

  • In Collaboration With: Rutgers Teaching and Learning with Technology
  • Project Date: Spring 2019

Every year Rutgers GRID creates a game for the RU Online Learning Conference. In 2019, the game created for the conference explored the extended realities. Users could scan their pamphlets to experience AR, take a 360 photo in the photobooth and view it in VR, and exchange challenge cards with other conference goers for an IRL networking activity.
