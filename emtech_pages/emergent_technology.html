Emergent Technology

The Office of University Online Education Services has specialized staff that explores, evaluates, and implements cutting-edge technological solutions to enhance online learning experiences. This forward-thinking group works closely with faculty and instructional designers to integrate innovative tools and platforms into fully online degree programs, ensuring that the university remains at the forefront of digital education and provides students with the most advanced and effective online learning environments.

Generative AI

Our staff helps identify and evaluate cutting-edge AI tools tailored to educational needs, develops custom AI applications to address specific challenges in online learning, and provides strategic advice on integrating AI into course design and delivery. Through workshops, consultations, and hands-on support, we empower administrators and instructors to harness the potential of AI, improving efficiency in program management and creating more personalized, engaging learning experiences for students in online programs.

Extended Reality (XR)

We can provide comprehensive support for integrating Extended Reality (XR) technologies into online courses, enhancing student engagement and learning outcomes. Our team offers guidance on selecting appropriate XR tools and platforms, assists in developing immersive learning experiences tailored to specific course objectives, and provides training on effectively implementing XR activities in online environments. Through hands-on workshops and personalized consultations, we empower instructors to create virtual labs, 3D simulations, and interactive scenarios that bring complex concepts to life, offering students unique, experiential learning opportunities in their online programs.

GRID Lab

The Gaming Research and Immersive Design (GRID) Lab is a cutting-edge space where online instructors can explore and experiment with a wide range of emergent technologies. This hands-on environment allows faculty to experience virtual reality, augmented reality, gaming platforms, and other innovative tools firsthand, fostering a deeper understanding of their potential applications in online education. By providing a dedicated space for exploration and creativity, the GRID Lab empowers instructors to envision new possibilities for their courses, ultimately leading to more engaging and immersive learning experiences for students in online programs.
